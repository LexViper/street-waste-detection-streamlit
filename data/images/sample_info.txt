Sample Images for Clean City Waste Detection

This folder should contain sample images for testing the waste detection system.
Recommended image types:
- Street scenes with litter
- Plastic bottles and containers
- Paper waste and cardboard
- Organic waste like food scraps
- Mixed waste scenarios

For testing purposes, you can add your own images here or download sample images from:
- Unsplash (search for "litter", "waste", "recycling")
- Pixabay (search for "garbage", "trash", "street cleaning")
- Your own photos of urban waste

Supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, BMP, TIFF

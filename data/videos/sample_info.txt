Sample Videos for Clean City Waste Detection

This folder should contain sample videos for testing the waste detection system.
Recommended video types:
- Street cleaning operations
- Urban environments with visible litter
- Time-lapse of waste accumulation
- Recycling center footage
- Park or public space monitoring

For testing purposes, you can add your own videos here.
Supported formats: MP4, AVI, MOV

Note: Video processing in the current implementation samples frames for analysis.
Full video processing can be implemented for production use.
